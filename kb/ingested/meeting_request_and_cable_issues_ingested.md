# Meeting Request and Cable Issues

## Issue Pattern
Many requests for meeting setup, laptop connectivity, printer issues, and hardware installation troubleshooting. Some users are experiencing difficulties with their peripherals, such as laptops, monitors, printers, and routers.

## Common Symptoms
- Meetings not set up correctly
- Laptops connected but unable to establish a connection
- Printer issues, including duplex unit not set or device recovery reports
- Hardware installation problems, including router replacements and monitor installations

## Possible Causes
- Misconfigured meeting requests
- Loose connections on laptops or peripherals
- Incorrect printer settings or device driver issues
- Hardware malfunctions or incomplete installations

## Troubleshooting Steps

1. Check the meeting request for any incorrect settings.
2. Verify that all cables are securely connected to their respective devices.
3. Update printer drivers and ensure duplex unit is set correctly.
4. Check hardware installation instructions carefully and follow them step-by-step.

## Escalation Team
The Network Infrastructure team will be responsible for resolving complex hardware-related issues, while the IT Service Desk team will handle meeting setup requests and general troubleshooting.

## Priority Guidance
This issue is considered Low priority unless it affects critical business operations or requires immediate attention from a senior engineer.
