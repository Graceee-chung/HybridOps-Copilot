# Access Issues and Troubleshooting Guide

## Issue Pattern
Common problem: Access issues to various systems (storage, HR, testing) with varying levels of severity.

## Common Symptoms
Symptoms:
* Users unable to access certain systems or folders
* Permission errors or denied access
* Confusion around user roles and responsibilities
* Difficulty in managing access lists

## Possible Causes
Possible causes:
* Inaccurate or outdated user information
* Misconfigured access controls
* Insufficient permissions or rights
* Human error in managing access

## Troubleshooting Steps

1. **Verify User Information**: Ensure that user account information is accurate and up-to-date.
2. **Check Access Controls**: Review access control lists and ensure that users have the correct level of permission.
3. **Investigate Permission Conflicts**: Look for potential conflicts between different permissions or roles.
4. **Monitor System Activity**: Keep an eye on system activity logs to identify any unusual patterns.
5. **Clear Cache and Cookies**: Clear cache and cookies in affected systems.

## Escalation Team
The Access Team is the most likely team to assist with these issues.

## Priority Guidance

* Low priority: Issues related to testing or development environments.
* Medium priority: Issues requiring review of access controls or user information.
* High priority: Critical access issues that impact business operations.
