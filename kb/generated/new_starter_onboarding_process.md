# New Starter Onboarding Process

## Issue Pattern
The common problem in new starter onboarding is the incomplete or missing documentation, forms, and information required for their induction and employment.

## Common Symptoms
* Empty return form attached to some starter forms
* Missing or incomplete start date
* Incomplete or missing paperwork related to employment contracts and access cards
* Difficulty accessing contractor access card or permanent pass

## Possible Causes
* Human error during initial onboarding process
* Lack of standardization in documentation and paperwork
* Insufficient communication between teams

## Troubleshooting Steps

1. Review the attached forms and documents for completeness and accuracy.
2. Verify the start date with the candidate to ensure it is accurate.
3. Check if all required paperwork has been submitted and completed.
4. Ensure that the contractor access card or permanent pass has been issued correctly.

## Escalation Team
The HR Support team is recommended as the escalation point for this issue, particularly when dealing with incomplete or missing documentation.

## Priority Guidance

* Low priority: For issues related to minor paperwork discrepancies or minor communication errors.
* Medium priority: For issues that require attention from a higher authority but do not have significant consequences, such as incorrect start dates.
* High priority: For issues that have serious implications for the candidate's employment or access to resources.
