# New Purchase PO Support

## Issue Pattern
The common problem in this issue pattern is related to the processing and allocation of new purchases, including retrieving old devices, tracking receipt numbers, and allocating items to users.

## Common Symptoms
* Received a new purchase PO with no update on device allocation or item delivery.
* Unable to retrieve information on old devices after receiving new items.
* Missing or outdated receipts for received items.
* Inconsistent allocation of items to users.

## Possible Causes
* Human error in processing purchase orders.
* Systemic issues with tracking receipt numbers or item delivery.
* Lack of communication between teams involved in the process (e.g., procurement, IT, and customer support).

## Troubleshooting Steps

1. **Log in to the system**: Access the purchase order management system to retrieve detailed information on the current PO.
2. **Check allocation status**: Verify if the item has been allocated to a user and check for any discrepancies or updates.
3. **Retrieve old device info**: Use the receipt number to retrieve information on the old device, including its current location and allocation status.
4. **Track receipt numbers**: Ensure that all receipts are up-to-date and accurately recorded in the system.
5. **Communicate with teams**: Collaborate with relevant teams (e.g., procurement, IT) to resolve any outstanding issues or discrepancies.

## Escalation Team
The most likely team for escalation is the IT Support Team.

## Priority Guidance

* This issue should be classified as Low priority if all steps above are completed successfully.
* If any of the troubleshooting steps fail, or if there are inconsistencies in the allocation process, this issue may need to be escalated to a Medium priority level.
* In cases where critical items are missing or delayed, this issue may require immediate attention and should be escalated to High priority.
